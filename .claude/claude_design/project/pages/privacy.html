Privacy policy

We keep your data minimal, secure and yours. Here’s exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Information we collect

We collect what you give us — name, email, phone and your chosen exam — plus learning activity such as classes watched and tests attempted, which powers your study plan.

How we use it

To personalise your plan, run your classes and tests, process payments, and send you account and progress updates. We do not sell your data, ever.

Sharing

We share data only with processors who help us operate (payments, hosting, email) under strict confidentiality, and when required by law.

Your choices

You can view, export or delete your data anytime from your account, or by writing to privacy@xtopr.in. Unsubscribe links sit on every email.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with access limited to staff who need it to support you.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email privacy@xtopr.in and we’ll respond within 72 hours.
